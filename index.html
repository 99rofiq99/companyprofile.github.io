About Trijaya Coffee

We are a company focusing on export and import of coffee products. We prioritize high quality of our products and services. We regularly check and control of our product in order to be able to provide the best quality products to our customers.

In running our business, we uphold ethics and integrity so that not only do we look for profit, but also we ensure that there is a mutual trust between clients and the company. We are here to help our customers solve their problems.

We are able to supply both arabica and robusta green beans up to 80 MT per month. We ensure the product will meet the specification all our customers demanded. We work hard to satisfy what we are demanded, best quality and professional service.

What we do

Product research

We do researches in order to provide our customers the best coffee products. We always try our best to ensure that our customers receive the highest quality of products and services and meet the expectation they have.

Quality Control

We control and secure our product quality. Our principal value is the commitment to provide the highest quality possible.

Customer Care

Not only do we sell the highest quality products, but we also exist to help our customers solve their problems. We believe that a good and sustainable business starts from a good relationship. Hence, we maintain a good relationship with our customers to create a great and healthy business environment.
